FAQ

Common Questions

Do you accept walk-ins?

We recommend booking in advance to avoid waiting, but we do accommodate walk-ins if slots are available. Emergency cases are always seen same-day — call ahead.

What payment options do you offer?

We accept cash, all major cards, UPI, and offer 0% EMI on treatments above ₹3,000. We also work with most dental insurance providers.

Is the first consultation really free?

Yes — your initial consultation, oral examination, and treatment recommendation are completely free of charge, with no obligation to proceed.

How long does a typical appointment take?

A routine cleaning takes 45–60 minutes. Complex procedures vary — we'll give you a clear time estimate when you book so you can plan your day.

Do you treat children?

Absolutely. We see children from age 3 and have a dedicated pediatric approach — gentle, patient, and designed to build positive dental habits early.

What if I have dental anxiety?

Tell us when you book — we have specific protocols for anxious patients including slower pacing, explain-before-you-do communication, and comfort options.
